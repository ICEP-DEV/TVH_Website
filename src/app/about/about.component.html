About

What The Hack?

Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is a program that is meant to challenge the Information and
Communication Technology (ICT) students across learning institutions in Tshwane Metro to
demonstrate their skills in software development, cloud computing and agile practices while
competing to address industry and community problems through technology.

The 4th annual hackathon will take a different shape in 2022 when compared to hackathon
we had in the previouse years. This year the TVH organizing team has prioritised the two
primary objectives of the hackathonprogram which are:

• To have an extension of the hackathon program to take longer with build-up "Sprints"
N.B. A sprint in a Scrum is a short period of time wherein a development
team works to complete specific tasks, milestones, or deliverables. This
way we'll accomodate needed training for some of the hackers before
implementing their ideas through out the recommended technologies
and methods.

• To establish TVH identity that promotes creativity by calling for a design of the TVH
brand for every editional sprint.

3 days, 4 nights Code

Groups of 2-5 individuals will form around a project team, such as building a new data visualization, writing a document, or collaboratively investigating a problem. Participants will connect virtual and get working. Hacking will begin with project introductions.
